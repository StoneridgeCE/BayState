Executive Summary

Bay State Milling is designing three major customer-facing processes in D365 Customer Engagement. Although the business outcomes differ, each process benefits from a common operating model: initiate and orchestrate work in CE, apply scenario-specific validations and approvals, use specialized teams or HighRadius where required, hand approved transactional work to D365 F&O, and return downstream status to CE for customer visibility, follow-up, and reporting.

Common Operating Model

D365 CEInitiate & OrchestrateIntake, context, classification, and required information captured at the source.
D365 CEValidate & ApproveScenario-specific validations, exception routing, and approvals.
SpecializedAssess Where RequiredHighRadius credit assessment, QA/R&D review, specialized teams.
D365 F&OExecute & PostTransactions, fulfillment, customer master, inventory, and accounting.
IntegrationReturn StatusReferences, statuses, and outcomes flow back to CE for visibility and reporting.

Processes in Scope

Case Management / CERT

Manage complaints, service issues, quality events, investigations, actions and related financial outcomes.

CE responsibility
Case intake, classification, risk, investigation, actions, communication, approvals, SLAs and reporting.
F&O / external
Posts credits, rebills, replacements, claims, write-offs or inventory transactions and returns status.

Sample Requests

Manage a sample from customer or internal need through validation, approval, fulfillment, delivery and outcome.

CE responsibility
Request intake, product/customer context, validations, exception approvals, visibility, follow-up and outcome.
F&O / external
Fulfillment, inventory, shipment, freight, billing, costing and item setup; R&D/QA support exceptions.

Customer Requests

Manage new customer setup and 15 additional credit or customer-maintenance scenarios.

CE responsibility
Request intake, classification, financial/non-financial routing, approvals, documents, status and reporting.
F&O / external
HighRadius manages external credit assessment; F&O creates the authoritative customer and completes activation.
